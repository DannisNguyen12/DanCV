About Me

My journey from Vietnam to Australia, and from healthcare to technology

I began my tech journey in Vietnam at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, where my strong academic performance earned me a scholarship to study at Western Sydney University in Australia. This opportunity opened doors to a world of possibilities and shaped my perspective on technology's global impact.

Beyond the classroom, I've worked across disciplines — from healthcare as a nursing assistant to education as a PASS Facilitator for programming and private tutor for math and chemistry. These diverse experiences brought empathy, communication skills, and logical thinking into my tech practice, making me not just a coder, but a problem-solver who understands human needs.

Whether leading study sessions or building software like EcoExchange and Restaurant Order Food Website, I bring curiosity, critical thinking, and persistence to every challenge. I'm passionate about solving real-world problems through code and turning innovative ideas into meaningful impact. I'm now ready to bring that energy and intelligence to a professional development team.
